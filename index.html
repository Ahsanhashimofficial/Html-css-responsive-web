About Us

Baker’s and Bakery was founded in 2018, with the vision of creating a baking raw material supply company that offered specialty ingredients rather than the traditional commodities of flour, sugar and salt. Bakers n Bakery provides high quality products related to baking raw material for industrial and commercial uses. We are constantly working to source those ingredients that will provide our customers with the latest trends in the industry. Product quality is our main focus to make sure our customers never have any issues. Delivering goodness all over Pakistan with many options for shipping and payments. We cater a great variety of products for baking and cake decoration from world class brands to local great quality stuff.

Description

Frozen Dough Improver

Description


An anti-flakes solution, a mix of enzymes and emulsifiers, designed to correct the flours for standard breads, raw and frozen dough, during bread-making process.


  • Resolves the main problem of frozen bread, eliminating crust flaking and preventing the formation of blisters.
  • Better defrosting and fermentation during baking process.
  • Boosts the tolerance of the dough for raw frozen dough.
  • Good resistance of the yeast at defrosting.
  • Improves extensibility and machinability.
  • Preserves freshness and gives a nice aspect to the bread.

Specification: FROZEN DOUGH SOLUTION – Multi purposes bread improver, same characteristics as a standard bread improver, just thawing process is required.

High Fat Process Butter

Description


High Fat process butter is typically made with dairy fats (50%) and hydrogenated vegetable shortening (50%), it has low oxidation rates and is the main element for baking crispy biscuits, crumbly pastry, cookies, wafers, analogue cheese and other food products instead of butter. It is used in baking as a fixative and moistening agent. It helps to hold batters together, grease baking pans and prevent sticking.
